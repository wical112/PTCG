Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

TCG Tournament Manager (the “Service”) is a free, browser-based tool. We believe in keeping things simple: your tournament data is yours, and it stays on your device.

2. What we store

  • Local storage (on your device only). Player names, round results, timer state, wheel history, language preference, and per-round snapshots are saved using your browser’s localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you intentionally export or share it (for example, by downloading the standings image).
  • No accounts. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • No server storage. The Service is fully static. There is no backend database receiving your tournament data.

3. Cookies

The Service itself does not set tracking cookies. If advertising is enabled in the future (see Section 5), third-party ad providers may set their own cookies, as disclosed in their own privacy policies.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (for example, aggregated pageview counts) to understand general usage. Any analytics tool used will be disclosed here and configured to avoid collecting personal data where possible.

5. Third-party advertising

If we enable third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense), those providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

6. Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data deletion

To delete all tournament data, use the Reset button inside the app, or clear site data for this domain in your browser’s settings.

8. International users

Because all data stays in your browser, no international data transfer occurs. If you are in the EU, UK, or other regions with data-protection laws, you retain full control of your data at all times.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact method listed on the About page.
