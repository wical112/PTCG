About

What it is

TCG Tournament Manager is a free, browser-based tool for running Swiss-system tournaments and related events. It handles player registration, pairing generation, round timing, result entry, standings with OWP tie-breaks, trainer-card match history, a lucky-draw wheel, and recovery from lost data.

Who it’s for

Card-game judges, local card shops, community organizers, and anyone who needs to run an event without wrestling with spreadsheets or paying for an account.

Design principles

  • Privacy first. Your data stays in your browser. No sign-up, no cloud, no tracking of tournament content.
  • Fast at the table. Designed to be operated with one hand on a phone or laptop while running a real event.
  • No lock-in. You can reset, export, or walk away at any time.
  • Bilingual. Every screen is available in English and Traditional Chinese.

How tie-breaks are calculated

The primary ranking is match points (3 for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss). Ties are broken using Opponent Win Percentage (OWP) with a 25% floor per opponent, consistent with common TCG conventions. Byes grant 3 match points but do not count toward OWP.
